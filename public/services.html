Clutch & Transmission

Axle shafts are commonly referred to as “CV axles” or “half-shafts.” An axle shaft is a narrow, pole-like part that connects a wheel to the gears in the transmission.

Axles are part of the system that enables the wheels to rotate.

One end of the axle shaft is connected to the transmission and the other end is connected to the wheel.

The transmission rotates the axle which rotates the wheels. Without the axles, the tires will not rotate and the car will not move.
